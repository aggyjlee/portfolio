Moonrise Social Club


As a long-time follower of @followmario's content on TikTok, I was surprised to come across Mario Moreno's TikTok about looking for interns at his newly founded marketing consulting company. The internship seemed up my alley, so I took a chance at applying by sending an email that night! Fortunately, I received a positive response from Mario who let me know that he grew excited to work with me as he read through my email. Over the course of six months, I conducted research on online marketing courses, created the presentation design and built out a portion of Mario's Marketing Foundations E-Learning Masterclass. The course launched on January 2nd at moonrisesocialclub.com.
