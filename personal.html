To further my knowledge in design, I frequently enroll in online courses taught by designers in the social media space and utilize my skills for my hobbies.

Personal Projects


As a long-time fan of the group NCT 127, I was asked to design a banner and a pack of pins that were printed out and distributed to over 2,000 fans at the concert. By posting the banner design and customized promotion material on Twitter, we were able to fundraise over $700 to pay for production costs. It was exciting as both a fan and designer to see so many fans use the banner during the concert and even the band member himself notice the design!

Banner for NCT 127's Neo City in Chicago concert

I also enjoy watching various compilation videos made by Korean fans because of the editing style that resembles popular Korean variety shows, so I edited something similar to use as practice in replicating this variety show editing style. Many music acts now produce a lot of YouTube and digital content, which I also used as inspiration when creating the graphics and captions in the video.
