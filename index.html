Say Hello.


My experience in social media marketing, design, video editing and content creation have been fostered through amazing internship opportunities at companies like Glantz Design and PBS Digital Studios. I'm also an active member in student publications like STITCH Magazine, North by Northwestern and The Daily Northwestern.

You can also find me creating lesson plans for and teaching the eleven students (1st - 10th grade) that I private tutor over Zoom. I've always loved working with kids and have consistently volunteered and worked at after school programs and tutor academies.

My other (very specific) interests include mommy vloggers, Yulhee, NCT, Lindt milk chocolate bars, researching online learning resources for design and video, falling into internet rabbit holes and the color yellow.
