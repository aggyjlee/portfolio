Hello! I am Agnes Jihyun Lee, a senior at Northwestern University in Evanston, IL. I am currently majoring in Journalism and minoring in Psychology. I am also pursuing the Integrated Marketing Communications certificate and completing the Digital Media module in the School of Communication. My intended graduation date is June 2022.


My experience in social media marketing, design, video editing and content creation have been fostered through amazing internship opportunities at companies like Glantz Design and PBS Digital Studios. I'm also an active member in student publications like STITCH Magazine, North by Northwestern and The Daily Northwestern.

You can also catch me creating lesson plans for the ten students (3rd - 10th grade) that I private tutor over Zoom. I've always loved working with kids and have consistently volunteered and worked at after school programs and tutor academies.

My other interests include mommy vloggers, Yulhee, NCT, Lindt milk chocolate bars, researching online learning resources for design and video, falling into internet rabbit holes and the color yellow.
